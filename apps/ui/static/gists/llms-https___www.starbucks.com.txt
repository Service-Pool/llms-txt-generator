# https://www.starbucks.com/

> Starbucks offers a wide range of coffee beverages, including seasonal specials and new menu items like the Iced Ube Coconut Macchiato and Iced Lavender Cream Matcha. Customers can conveniently order through their mobile app for pickup or delivery via partners, with options for in-store dining and refills. The site also details the company's history, rewards program, and various contact channels for customer service and inquiries.

## Pages

- [Starbucks Coffee Company](https://www.starbucks.com/): Starbucks introduces its revamped Starbucks Rewards program with new status levels and earning opportunities. The app facilitates easy ordering for pickup, especially for travelers, and highlights new spring beverages like the Iced Lavender Cream Matcha and Iced Ube Coconut Macchiato. Customers can also enjoy free refills on brewed coffee and tea when dining in.
- [Featured Spring Menu: Starbucks Coffee Company](https://www.starbucks.com/menu/featured/): The Starbucks spring menu features new items such as the Iced Ube Coconut Macchiato and Toasted Coconut Cream Cold Brew, alongside returning favorites like the Iced Lavender Cream Matcha. The menu also includes matcha variations, a Frog Cake Pop, Yuzu Citrus Blossom croissant-muffin, and high-protein beverage and food options like the Iced Sugar-Free Caramel Protein Latte and Bacon, Gouda & Egg Sandwich.
- [Easy Mobile & Online Ordering & Delivery: Starbucks Coffee Company](https://www.starbucks.com/stores-and-ordering): Starbucks offers multiple convenient ordering options, including classic cafes, drive-thrus, and delivery through partners like DoorDash and Uber Eats. Mobile Order and Pay via the Starbucks app allows for quick customization and pickup, with payment options extending to wearables. The company also has locations in airports, hotels, and grocery stores, alongside its premium Starbucks Reserve® and Roastery experiences.
- [About Us: Starbucks Coffee Company](https://www.starbucks.com/about-us): Starbucks began in 1971 in Seattle's Pike Place Market, initially selling coffee beans and spices. Inspired by Italian coffeehouses, the company evolved into a coffeehouse chain, expanding globally and focusing on nurturing human connection through every cup and conversation.
- [Contact Us: Starbucks Coffee Company](https://www.starbucks.com/contact): Starbucks provides multiple contact channels for customer service, including a Customer Service Site with FAQs, phone, and live chat. Specific contacts are available for media inquiries, investor relations, and partnership or sponsorship requests.
